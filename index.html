What We Do

A long-term relationship between an employee and your business is our goal. MyStaff.online has a proven track record of finding and supporting key staff. Our process of recruitment ensures your business has access to the best.

Search

With boot son the ground we leave no stone unturned in the quest to find the right employee for your business.

Interview

Background checks, verifying diplomas. English test and recorded video interviews. Test's can be conducted as well as sample works provided in this state.

Employ

We prepare and execute contracts on your behalf, removing your company from all liability. We also handle all the financial negotiations and legalities that will arise. At this stage we establish the key processes and expectations from the employers, ensuring the relationship starts on a solid foundation.

Support

We are there every step of the way. Handling any disputes or misunderstandings. We are your HR department.

Monitor

Through our portal, we can if you choose provide screenshots of your employees progress every 3minutes, monitor how many mouse movements and key strokes they are producing through out the day. We also have a team on the ground that regularly checks in with your staff.

Report

We can produce reports showing how many hours your employees took to complete given task providing you with the date you need to make informed decisions. We also provide a summary of what has been achieved and how you can achieve more from your staff. These report stake the fear and unknown out of hiring off-shore.

Our Software

Tracks & Reports

Strokes and mouse movements are recoded showing how productive they are being. It will help you and your team maximise productivity each day. It records your working hours and let's you manage your time spent for each task.
